Welcome to the OMEGA Games Team, we are the people in the company that bring you the latest information and maintain the website. Below is our team and some of their favourite games.
Omega Games was first set up in 2014 by a few senior web developers from various companies who wished to create a business that sole purpose was for the fans of gaming. Being gamers themselves they understand what was needed and would could benefit fellow gamers.
They first Omega Games building was in Nottingham, England. In 2019 our latest building is in Los Angeles, California an amazing feat in just 5 years the company has expanded from England to France to the United States.
The company consists of over 700 employees ranging from web developers, web researchers, product managers and many more. This prestigious company has won many awards and certifications throughout the years and no doubt it will win many more in the years to come.
